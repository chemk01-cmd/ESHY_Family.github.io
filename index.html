About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher interested in the molecular and cellular basis of neurodegenerative diseases, including Alzheimer's disease and related disorders. My research integrates transcriptomics, small RNA biology, and human cell-based models to identify disease mechanisms and clinically relevant biomarkers.
